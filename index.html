STORYBOARD

HackCville's Digital Media Program

A CLASS FOR THE

DIGITAL STORYTELLER

"I love the new people HackCville exposed me to: people who are driven, creative, ambitious, and inspirational. I also loved the skills, both hard and soft, I got to learn and practice."

- Becky Ross, Storyboard Program Graduate

Develop skills and apply them through hands-on experience

Storyboard is a 12-week program that teaches video production and digital storytelling. Students learn skills in interviewing, filming, and editing and publish their work to HackCville’s publication, The Pioneer.

See it in action

Storyboard is a 12-week program that teaches video production and digital storytelling. Students learn skills in interviewing, filming, and editing and publish their work to HackCville’s publication, The Pioneer.

See it in action

Watch your work come alive in the Adobe Creative Suite and tackle concepts such as binaural audio and animations. A cumulative final project will combine and make use of all of the topics learned in Storyboard.
